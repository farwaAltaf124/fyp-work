Frequently asked question (FAQs)

What is required to get started using DGIEIE webcam services?

An H.264 network camera (webcam), a dedicated internet connection, a place to mount the webcam and a power source (utility or solar power) to power the camera.

What devices can I stream too?

DGIEIE network was designed and built to stream to virtually any device - Computer, Smart Phone / Tablet ( Android),

Who monitors my webcam and live stream(s)?

DGIEIE managed webcam streaming services include webcam and network monitoring. Our monitoring servers are configured to monitor your webcam making sure it is online. If the camera goes offline, alerts are sent via email and/or text message to your cell phone letting you know an issue exists.

What is required to host a live webcam at my location?

A typical camera installation requires four components – Power, Dedicated Internet, Network Connection and Mounting Location.

  • Power – All webcam hardware solutions require power of some sort – either utility power or off-grid solar/wind. 24VAC,or Power Over Ethernet are typical power delivery options.
  • Dedicated Internet – A dedicated internet connection of at least 500Kbps upload speed is required to stream live video using DGIEIE services.
  • Network Connection – A network connection from the camera to the internet router is required. This connection can be over wired Ethernet, WIFI or long range fixed wireless technology.
  • Mounting –Whether mounting to an existing pole, wall, ceiling, parapet, on a rooftop or if no mounting surface exists, DGIEIE hardware solutions include mounting adapters to facilitate the installation. If your not taking advantage of our installation services, our hardware solutions are designed to be as simple as possible for self-install.

Do you support H.264 cameras?

Yes – Our network supports most all H.264 network IP cameras including those made by the Hikvision manufacturer .

What speed internet connection is required to stream live video?

Faster upload speeds equate to higher quality live video streaming. DGIEIE can setup your live video streaming service with a minimum of 384Kbps upload speed. Video resolution, video frame rate, compression and the amount of motion in the video are all factors that determine the required upload speed to support live video streaming.

Is it a problem to move the webcam once it is installed?

No. You are free to move your webcam at any time to a different location provided the necessary power and network connection to your internet router are in place.

How do I change or edit my camera details ?

User will be able to change or update their camera settings by filling up user registration form.

How to resolve user queries?

User will be able to get answers to their queries through contact us form in which they will be asked to provide email and further contact details so that our support team will reach to them.

Supported Browsers?

DGI-EIE Network Cameras are best viewed with a modern HTML5 web browser such as Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Opera, Microsoft Edge, and Safari.
