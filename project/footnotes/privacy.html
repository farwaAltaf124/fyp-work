DGI-EIE Privacy Policies

DGI-EIE's policy is to respect and protect the privacy of our users. DGI-EIE takes your privacy concerns seriously and firmly supports protecting your privacy online. DGI-EIE will never deliberately reveal private information about our users to any third party without first receiving that user's consent.

Information you provide to Us

DGI-EIE collects information that personally identifies you and other personal information about you or users whose information you provide to us to authenticate users and to send notifications to those users relating to DGI-EIE ("Personal Information"). We do this when you submit a camera site to DGI-EIE, sign up for DGI-EIE's.The user's contact information is used to allow the user to utilize DGI-EIE features, contact the user when necessary, and advise them about new services, products, and/or software upgrades.

Reporting a Violation

DGI-EIE provides users with the ability to report a violation they find on our websites by filling out and submitting the DGI-EIE Contact Us form.

Keeping your data in one place

We promise not to distribute, lease or sell any of the data you supply to DGI-EIE to any third parties unless with your permission or if we are required by law to do so.

Removing your data

Upon request, when you're cancelling your account, we will permanently remove any stored data that potentially could be used for identifying you.

You can change your personal information

By Filling up contact us form and by chatting with us.
